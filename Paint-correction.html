What is Paint Correction?

Paint correction is the process of removing imperfections from your vehicle’s paintwork, such as:

  • Swirl Marks
  • Fine Scratches
  • Water Spots
  • Oxidation
  • Holograms from Improper Buffing

Using specialized compounds and polishing techniques, our expert technicians carefully restore your vehicle’s paint to a smooth and glossy finish. The result is a car that not only looks new again but also feels silky smooth to the touch.

The Paint Correction Process

  • Comprehensive Inspection: We begin by assessing your vehicle’s paint to determine the level of correction required.
  • Decontamination: A clay bar treatment is used to remove contaminants such as dirt, brake dust, and industrial fallout from the surface of the paint.
  • Polishing: Our technicians use machine polishers and a range of cutting compounds to remove surface imperfections. This multi-stage process ensures every imperfection is buffed out.
  • Refining: Once the imperfections are removed, we use a fine polish to enhance the depth and clarity of the paint.
  • Protection: To maintain the newly corrected paint, we recommend applying our Ceramic Coating, which provides long-term protection and a high-gloss finish.

Benefits of Paint Correction

  • Increased Value: Paint correction can add significant value to your vehicle, making it more attractive to potential buyers if you decide to sell.
