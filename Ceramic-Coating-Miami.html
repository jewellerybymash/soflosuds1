What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle’s paintwork that chemically bonds with the surface to create a durable, protective layer. This advanced coating offers:

  • Enhanced Gloss: Provides a deep, high-gloss finish that enhances the appearance of your vehicle.
  • Hydrophobic Properties: Repels water, making it easier to clean and reducing water spots.
  • UV Protection: Shields your paint from harmful UV rays that can cause oxidation and fading.
  • Chemical Resistance: Protects against harmful chemicals, such as road salts and acidic contaminants.

The Ceramic Coating Process

  • Preparation: We begin with a thorough wash and decontamination of your vehicle to ensure the surface is clean and free of impurities.
  • Paint Correction: Any existing imperfections, such as scratches and swirl marks, are corrected to ensure the best possible bonding of the ceramic coating.
  • Application: The ceramic coating is carefully applied by hand, covering every inch of your vehicle’s paint. Multiple layers may be applied for enhanced protection and durability.
  • Curing: The coating is allowed to cure properly, forming a strong, protective bond with the paint. This typically involves a curing time of 24-48 hours.

Benefits of Ceramic Coating

  • Long-Lasting Protection: Ceramic coatings provide a durable layer of protection that lasts for years, compared to traditional waxes and sealants.
  • Easier Maintenance: With its hydrophobic properties, your vehicle will stay cleaner for longer, and washing will become quicker and easier.
  • Enhanced Durability: The coating shields your paint from environmental hazards such as UV rays, acid rain, and road debris.
  • Improved Resale Value: A well-maintained, protected vehicle often commands a higher resale value.
